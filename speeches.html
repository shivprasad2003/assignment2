"Sisters and Brothers of America" – Chicago, 1893

Swami Vivekananda delivered his iconic speech at the World’s Parliament of Religions in Chicago on September 11, 1893. His opening words, "Sisters and Brothers of America"**, received a standing ovation. He emphasized religious tolerance, acceptance, and universal brotherhood**.
